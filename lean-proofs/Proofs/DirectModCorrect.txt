It is known that, for integers x, y, z ∈ Z: (xy mod z) = ((x mod z)(y mod z)) mod z. (1) Assume toward direct proof that (a mod m) = (b mod m), a, b ∈ Z. Then we have a2, b2 ∈ Z, and from Eq. 1 and our premise we find (a3 mod m) in terms of b as follows: a2 mod m = ((a mod m)(a mod m)) mod m = ((b mod m)(b mod m)) mod m = b2 mod m a3 mod m = (a · a2) mod m = ((a mod m)(a2 mod m)) mod m = ((b mod m)(b2 mod m)) mod m = b · b2 mod m = b3 mod m.
